About Us

About PT Citra Gemilang Prima

Founded in Jakarta in 2003, CGP is the trusted gateway for premium international food brands entering the Indonesian market. With a dedicated team of industry professionals, we provide end-to-end supply chain management — from import documentation to last-mile delivery across Indonesia's modern trade landscape.

  • Indonesian Customs & Import Expertise Full compliance management — permits, certifications, BPOM, and Halal clearance handled in-house.
  • Seamless Nationwide Distribution Network Established relationships with all major modern trade retailers across Java, Bali, and beyond.
  • Over 20 Years of B2B Relationships Deep, trusted partnerships with both international brand principals and Indonesian retail buyers.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

"To be Indonesia's most trusted gateway for premium international food brands."

We aspire to be the definitive bridge between globally recognised food manufacturers and Indonesia's premium retail landscape — built on integrity, compliance excellence, and lasting trade partnerships.

Our Mission

  • Connect the world's finest food brands with Indonesia's premium modern trade channels.
  • Ensure full regulatory compliance — BPOM, Halal certification, and customs — for every product we distribute.

  • Deliver reliable, nationwide supply chain solutions that create sustainable value for all stakeholders.

Kanokwan Food

Over 30 years of determination to build Thailand's most recognised curry paste brand. Kanokwan's products are made using authentic recipes passed down through generations — now exported to over 40 countries worldwide. CGP is the exclusive Indonesian distributor for the full Kanokwan range.

A wholly owned subsidiary of the J.R. Simplot Company — one of North America's largest private agribusiness corporations. Their management team is committed to delivering nutritious food products that represent quality, value and convenience.
